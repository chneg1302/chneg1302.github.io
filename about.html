Why Chess for All?

A welcoming space for every chess player.

Inclusive Tournaments

Designed for beginners, students, working adults, and experienced competitors across different age groups. Our events are welcoming, well-structured, and open to all.

Learn While You Play

Improvement comes through experience. Alongside our regular tournaments, we offer coaching lessons and guided sessions to help you sharpen your skills and gain confidence.

Community First

Chess for All is more than an organiser — it is a community. Meet like-minded players, learn from mentors, and enjoy chess together.

Our Mission

To make chess accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone in Singapore. We believe that chess is a game for all ages and skill levels, and we're committed to creating an inclusive environment where every player can grow, compete, and connect with others who share their passion.

Through our tournaments and coaching programs, we aim to foster a vibrant chess community that celebrates learning, sportsmanship, and the joy of the game.

Our Vision

To become Singapore's leading community-driven chess organization, where players of all backgrounds feel welcome to participate, improve, and belong. We envision a future where chess brings people together, builds lasting friendships, and creates opportunities for personal growth and achievement.

We strive to be recognized not just for our tournaments and coaching, but for the strong, supportive community we've built—one that celebrates every player's journey and progress.
